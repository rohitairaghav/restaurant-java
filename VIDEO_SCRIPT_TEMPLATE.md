## Production Notes

### Recording Tips
1. **Use screen recording software** (OBS, Camtasia, Loom)
2. **Record in 1080p or higher** for clarity
3. **Use a clear, calm voice** for narration
4. **Highlight cursor movements** for clarity
5. **Add captions** for accessibility
6. **Keep videos under 10 minutes** for engagement

### Editing Tips
1. **Add intro/outro** with branding
2. **Add chapter markers** for easy navigation
3. **Highlight important actions** with zoom or annotations
4. **Add text overlays** for key points
5. **Include error handling** examples
6. **Show both desktop and mobile** views when relevant

### Distribution
1. **Upload to YouTube** or Vimeo
2. **Embed in documentation** website
3. **Link from application** help menu
4. **Create playlist** for easy access
5. **Add to onboarding** materials
